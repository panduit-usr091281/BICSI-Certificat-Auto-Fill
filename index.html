Upload your spreadsheet and certificate template to generate filled certificates automatically.

🔒 All processing happens in your browser. No data leaves your machine.

? How to Use This Tool

Before you begin, make sure you have two files ready on your computer:

📄 Certificate Template (.docx)

  1. Open your certificate template in Microsoft Word.
  2. Where the attendee's name should appear, type [name].
  3. Where the organization should appear, type [organization].
  4. Where the date should appear, type [date].
  5. Save the file as a .docx (Word Document).

💡 You can use different placeholder text — just make sure it matches what you enter in Step 2 below.

📊 Attendee Spreadsheet (.xlsx)

  1. Open Microsoft Excel and create a new workbook (or use an existing one).
  2. Add column headers in Row 1 — for example: Name, Organization, Date of Attendance.
  3. Fill in one row per attendee starting in Row 2.
  4. Save the file as .xlsx (Excel Workbook).

💡 Column names can be anything you like. You will match them to the right fields in Step 2.

🎓 Generating Your Certificates

  1. In Step 1 below, click each box to select your spreadsheet and your template from your computer.
  2. In Step 2, the tool will show a preview of your data. Use the dropdowns to tell it which column is the name, organization, and date. Verify the placeholder text matches your template.
  3. In Step 3, choose your output format (PDF, DOCX, or both) and click Generate Certificates.
  4. When the progress bar finishes, click Download All Certificates (ZIP) to save them to your computer.
